About Our Project

AgriGuard is an AI-powered plant disease detection system designed to help farmers and agricultural professionals identify crop diseases quickly and accurately. Using advanced deep learning technology, our system analyzes plant images to detect various diseases across multiple crop types.

Our mission is to empower farmers with cutting-edge technology that enables early disease detection, reduces crop losses, and promotes sustainable agricultural practices.
